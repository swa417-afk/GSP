# Glass Substrate Protocol (GSP)
## Standards Submission Cover Memo

This submission presents the Glass Substrate Protocol (GSP), an open, hardware‑enforced
governance architecture for accountable AI systems. GSP is designed for modular adoption,
independent certification, and standards alignment without reliance on founder authority.

The materials are intentionally structured for review by standards bodies including NIST and IEEE.
Normative requirements are clearly separated from non‑normative reference implementations.
